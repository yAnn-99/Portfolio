Who
Am I

I'm Yanis, an 18-year-old developer based in Paris, France, studying at Epitech Paris. I fell in love with code because it's the closest thing to Magic — turning nothing into something people actually use and enjoy.

My philosophy? Make everything easier for everyone, every day — whether that's a cleaner UI, a smarter script, or an automated setup that saves someone an hour. I believe good software is invisible: it just works, beautifully.

When I'm not coding, I'm deep in my Arch Linux rice or watching Valorant pro series. I used to compete myself at a semi-professional tier 3 level — esports taught me discipline, team communication, and how to perform under pressure. Skills that translate pretty well to dev work.

Right now I'm actively looking for a web dev internship (minimum 1 month) to grow, contribute, and prove myself in a real environment.
